# Basic IT Risk Assessment

## Scope
This document identifies common risks in a small-to-medium
enterprise IT environment.

---

## Identified Risks

### 1. Phishing Attacks
- Risk: Credential theft
- Impact: High
- Mitigation: User awareness training, email filtering, MFA

### 2. Unpatched Systems
- Risk: Exploitation of known vulnerabilities
- Impact: High
- Mitigation: Patch management and regular updates

### 3. Unauthorized Access
- Risk: Data breach
- Impact: High
- Mitigation: Least privilege, strong authentication, logging

### 4. Hardware Theft or Loss
- Risk: Data exposure
- Impact: Medium
- Mitigation: Disk encryption, asset tracking

---

## Conclusion
Regular risk assessments help organizations proactively identify
and reduce security threats before incidents occur.
